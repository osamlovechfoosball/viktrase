About

A practical water and sewer contractor for routes, networks and project sites.

VIK Trase focuses on external water supply and sewer networks, repairs, route replacement and larger site work.

Approach

We do not promise a price without inspection when the site needs assessment.

Quality execution starts with understanding the terrain. We clarify access, depth, existing connections, materials, timelines and actual scope before a specific offer.

What should be clear

  • what the task is
  • how the site can be accessed
  • what materials are required
  • what is included in the scope
  • what field risks exist
  • what timeline is realistic

Principles

Clear communication and execution based on real conditions.

Technical judgment

Solutions are shaped by route, terrain, access, materials and future use.

Clear scope

Before work starts, the task and included activities are clarified.

Practical execution

The approach is focused on a stable result on site, not an easy phone promise.

Have a route, repair or larger water and sewer project?

Send a location, photos and a short description. We will provide a practical first direction and arrange inspection if needed.
